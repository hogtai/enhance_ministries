Annual Fundraiser

2026 Golf Event

July 20, 2026 · Pheasant Hills Golf Course, Hammond, WI

Join us for a beautiful day of golf at one of western Wisconsin's great courses in support of Enhance Ministries' work with pastors and their churches.

This is sure to be a great day of golf and fellowship all for a great cause! Put a great team together and be sure to bring some extra cash because this year we will again be doing Strings, Tosses, and Mulligans!

Can't make it to golf? Feel free to leave an "I Can't Make It" donation below.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does my registration fee cover?

18 holes of golf, cart, lunch, dinner and prizes!

Can anyone register for golf?

Of course! It's about the fun and fundraising for our great ministry.

What if I don't have golf clubs?

Let us know and we'll take care of you.

Do I need to take the entire day off work?

No, the 11:30 am start to registration and 1 pm shotgun start are designed to allow those who need to get things done in the morning to do so before heading to the course.

Are there any skills competitions or prizes?

We'll have the traditional longest-drive, closest to the pin, and longest putt along with prizes for the winning team.

Is lunch included?

Yes, we'll have a quick lunch from the clubhouse available during registration.
